Planets

Planets are celestial bodies that orbits a star. Our solar system contains eight major planets, each of them with unique characteristics and environments.

Stars

Stars are massive glowing spheres made out of hot gases. They produce light and heat through nuclear fusion and are the building blocks of galaxies.

Galaxies

Galaxies are enormous systems made up of billions of stars, gas, dust, and dark matter, all they held together by gravity.
